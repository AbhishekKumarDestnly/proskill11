Fair Play Policy – ProSkill11

Last Updated: 17 March 2026

ProSkill11 is committed to maintaining a fair, transparent, and skill-based environment. This policy ensures equal opportunity and prevents misuse of the platform.

1. Skill-Based Platform

  • Outcomes depend on user knowledge and strategy
  • No gambling, betting, or chance-based system
  • Participation is free

2. Equal Opportunity

  • All users play under the same rules
  • No unfair advantage is given
  • Scoring is based on real match data

3. Prohibited Activities

  • Multiple accounts
  • Using bots or automation
  • Collusion between users
  • Manipulating leaderboards
  • Fake or misleading data

4. Monitoring & Enforcement

  • Accounts are monitored for suspicious activity
  • Violations may lead to suspension or permanent ban
  • Decisions by ProSkill11 are final

5. Leaderboard Integrity

  • Scores are calculated using verified data
  • Errors will be corrected if detected

6. Fair Usage

  • No abusive or harmful behavior
  • Respect all users
  • Play responsibly

7. Rewards Policy

  • Rewards are performance-based
  • No guaranteed rewards
  • Fraud may lead to reward cancellation

8. Reporting Issues

Users can report violations via support@proskill11.com

9. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time.
